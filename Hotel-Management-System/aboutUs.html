Who We Are

A booking experience built around the traveller

Bluebird was founded in 2019 with a single conviction: booking a hotel should feel as good as the stay itself. We set out to build a platform where real-time availability, honest reviews, and seamless payments come together in one intuitive space.

From our headquarters in Kuala Lumpur, our team of 200+ engineers, designers, and hospitality experts powers hotel discovery across 85 countries — serving both independent wanderers and enterprise travel managers with equal care.

We are registered with the Malaysian Tourism Board and hold full PCI-DSS compliance, so every booking you make is backed by institutional-grade security and industry accountability.

What Drives Us

Vision, Mission & Core Values

Our Vision

To become the most trusted travel accommodation platform in Southeast Asia — and eventually the world — by making every stay discoverable, bookable, and memorable, regardless of budget or destination.

Our Mission

To deliver precision, transparency, and reliability across every booking journey — connecting travellers to verified properties through technology that anticipates their needs and simplifies every decision from search to check-out.

Our Pledge

We pledge to put traveller wellbeing above commercial pressure — surfacing the best-fit stay, not the highest-margin one. Our ranking algorithms are audit-ready and our fee structures are always disclosed upfront.
